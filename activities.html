Exhibits

Some of my work/collaboration(s) meeting audiences in beautiful spaces.

Planets of Belief

For Science Gallery Bengaluru's exhibition season 'PSYCHE', I created this piece (paper-cutting artwork illuminated in a dark setting) as a part of the exhibit 'Change my Mind'. The exhibit connects physics and psychology - the way planets form and the way humans form memories and experience reality. Andrew Carnie from the University of Southampton, England and I put this together in an effort to intrigue the audience and scientists to think about neural implants in the light of helping people with disabilities (I would be more than happy to elaborate, please reach out for the same.

On the origins of life and meaning

For the Geneva Observatory's Science & Fiction project, I collaborated with Pedro Suassane (of the University of Geneva's Art division) to help create this thought-provoking piece.
